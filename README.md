# Ramappa Junior & Defence College Hanamkonda

Ramappa Junior & Defence College is the top intermediate college in Warangal and Hanamkonda, Telangana. We offer high-quality education for MPC, BiPC, CEC, and MEC groups integrated with intensive training for IIT-JEE, NEET, and specialized Defence Academy coaching (NDA, Army, Navy, Air Force) led by retired Army officers. We also feature the job-oriented HCL TechBee program.
